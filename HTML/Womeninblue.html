    Match Overview:

    On 2nd November 2025 ICC Women's world cup 2025 the final was held between india and south africa. "It is the 13th edition of women's world cup",the match was held at Dr.DY Patil Stadium,Navi Mumbai In that match india women team won the trophy by beating south africa women by 52 runs.This is the first trophy for india in ODI World cup.

    The match started 2 hours later because of the rain it was scheduled to start at 3pm but started at 5pm,the toss was tossed at 4.30 ISD, South africa won the toss and chose to bowl first after entering first into batting india scored 298 for the loss of 7 wickets, at the maximum shafali verma scored 87(78) including 7 fours and 2 sixes followed by deepti sharma 58(58) including 3 fours and a six. while in the south africa bowling ayabonga khaka took 3 wickets by giving 58 runs in 9 overs.

    South africa entered the field after the innings break with a target of 299. from the beginning they started losing wickets in a constant time interval but the captain laura wolvaardt stood and played a very nice innings she scored 101(98) including 11 fours and a six but the innings unfortunaltely didn't help south africa win the match no one except her played a very well innings all the other players dismissed in some decent and low runs.while in india bowling deepti sharma took 5 wickets by giving 39 runs in 9.3 overs and other bowlers bowled a economical spells at the end of the second innings south africa lost all wickets for 246 runs in 45.3 overs

    Shafali verma who scored 87 runs became the player of the match and deepti sharma who took 22 wickets and scored 215 runs in batting secured the player of the tournament award.
